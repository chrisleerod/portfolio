MOTION DESIGN

BotBroker

I was hired to create a motion graphic for BotBroker, a software selling platform, to reveal their newly developed site involving complex front-end and back-end systems to help facilitate safe transactions for those looking to purchase software. The project was completed with a deadline of two weeks in collaboration with their team. The video itself features animations that focus on showcasing the user-friendly site and functionality.

Role: Creative Director, Animator

Timeline: May 10 - May 24 2019

Project Overview

The main goal and purpose of this project were to illustrate an easy-to-use website, along with offering a user-experience focused marketplace. The animation itself features advanced design elements and mock user-interface designs. Not only was the project aimed at potential buyers, but also sellers.

Audience: Potential Customers
