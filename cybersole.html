MOTION DESIGN

Cybersole

I was hired to create a motion graphic for Cybersole, a software company, to reveal their robust update along with a completely revamped user-interface. This was completed over the course of seven days in collaboration with their CEO, Lucas, and Creative Director, Neal. The video itself features animations that focus on showcasing the many new features.

Role: Creative Director, Animator

Timeline: June 3 - June 10 2019

Project Overview

This project had one purpose: to present a highly anticipated update that would be seen by hundreds of thousands of people in the highest quality possible. The video needed to give users an in-depth look at the new features along with a new companion app that would improve the user's experience. The video has since reached nearly 500,000 people.

Audience: Current Users & Potential Customers
