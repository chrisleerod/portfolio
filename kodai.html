MOTION DESIGN

Kodai

I was hired to create a motion graphic for KodaiAIO, a software company, to reveal their newly developed dashboard that goes hand-in-hand with their software. This was completed over the course of five weeks in collaboration with their team. The video itself features three-dimensional camera movements and animations that help demonstrate the capabilities of the dashboard.

Role: Creative Director, Animator

Timeline: September - October 2019

Project Overview

The purpose of this project was to demonstrate the capabilities and features of their newly developed dashboard. Overall, the animation needed to not only amplify the looks of the dashboard, but also allow the viewers to see the functionality.

Audience: Current Users & Potential Customers
